About the Festival

The Lesotho Cultural Tourism Festival was created to showcase the rich traditions of the Basotho people while promoting tourism in the Mountain Kingdom.

History

Since its launch in 2015, the festival has attracted thousands of visitors from across Africa and internationally.

Target Tourists

  • Cultural tourists
  • Adventure travelers
  • International visitors
  • Students and researchers
